About Us

The Furry Friends Dog Sanctuary is a registered non-profit organization that has been rescuing and re-homing dogs in need since 2003. We work to address the overpopulation problem and to promote responsible dog ownership.

All of the dogs who are received into our sanctuary are partnered with loving, dedicated parents who open their homes to these animals in need.

Every dog receives proper medical care while at our sanctuary. This will range from the basics (vaccinations, deworming, microchip, spay/neuter), while others will require more extensive care (anything from dental work, to corrective surgery, to treatment of skin conditions).

Our Mission

To provide a safe, permanent homes for neglected and unwanted dogs, while promoting the human animal bond through education and community outreach.

Our Values

    We recognize dogs as an integral part of the family unit and the human animal bond is the central concept for Furry Friends
    We demonstrate humane and ethical treatment of animals
    We are accountable, responsible and respectful
    We practice integrity and honesty
    We demonstrate compassion and respect for dogs and people
    We demonstrate teamwork through collaboration, cooperation and in building trusting networks within Saskatchewan
