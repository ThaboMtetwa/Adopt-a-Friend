Adopt-a-Friend

Adopt a Friend is the website of the Furry Friends Dog Sanctuary located in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. We operate a boarding house for surrendered dogs and an adoption program to help find new homes for these animals. Our aim is simple: to show the value of compassion, animal companionship, and the unique ways that we are all better together.

Drop-Off Locations

We have three volunteer drop-off locations across Saskatoon and Area in Confederation, Arbor Creek, and Martensville. These locations are peoples homes, so we ask that you message us on social media or email info@adopt-a-friend.com for exact addresses.
